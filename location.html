How to Reach

Multiple convenient ways to get to Saman Villa

By Air

Bandaranaike International Airport (CMB)

150 km | 2 hours drive

  • Private airport transfer available
  • Taxi services (approx. Rs. 10,000 - 15,000)
  • Uber/PickMe available

By Car

Southern Expressway Route

Via E01 Highway

  • From Colombo: Take Southern Expressway (E01)
  • Exit at Pinnaduwa/Baddegama
  • Follow signs to Galle/Thalpe
  • GPS Coordinates: 6.0060769, 80.2642188

By Train

Coastal Railway Line

Scenic coastal route

  • Nearest station: Galle Railway Station (10 km)
  • Trains from Colombo Fort (2.5 - 3 hours)
  • Tuk-tuk/taxi from station (Rs. 800 - 1,200)
